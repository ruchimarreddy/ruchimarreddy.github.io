About

I am a Graduate Researcher at Purdue University working on deep learning theory and empirical analysis of modern neural networks. My research combines theory-driven questions with large-scale experiments, with a focus on optimization, training stability, generalization, and cross-architecture behavior in models such as CNNs, vision transformers, and GPT-style networks.

I am also interested in research engineering roles where I can build robust evaluation pipelines, analyze model behavior, and contribute to scalable experimentation workflows.

Research Interests

Training Dynamics

Studying how deep neural networks learn over time and how optimization behavior differs across architectures and optimizers.

Generalization and Stability

Designing evaluation metrics and analysis pipelines to understand robustness, inductive bias, and reliable model behavior.

Neural Tangent Kernel Analysis

Exploring NTK alignment, eigenspectra, and label-kernel interactions to better explain convergence in modern deep learning systems.

Research Engineering

Building reproducible experimentation workflows in Python and PyTorch for model evaluation, debugging, and large-scale empirical studies.

Projects

NTK Alignment Across Modern Architectures

Led PyTorch-based experiments analyzing training dynamics and generalization across CNNs, vision transformers, and GPT-style models, with emphasis on optimization behavior and alignment trends.

Module-Level Analysis Pipelines

Built custom Functorch-based pipelines to track module-level behavior, including attention and MLP blocks, diagnose bottlenecks, and guide optimizer and hyperparameter choices.

Text-to-Image Generation via Transformers

Developed a transformer-based text-to-image generation system inspired by CogView, combining a custom VQ-VAE autoencoder and transformer model trained on MS COCO.

External Memory for Reinforcement Learning

Developed a notebook-based external memory structure for reinforcement learning in partially observable environments, improving reward and task efficiency.

Quantum Diffusion Denoising Probabilistic Model

Re-implemented QuDDPM using Qiskit and Python, leveraging low-depth quantum circuits and evaluating performance with Maximum Mean Discrepancy loss.

AI WhatsApp Bot

Built a chatbot prototype using FastAPI, Twilio WhatsApp Sandbox, and lightweight local language models to automate conversational replies through a webhook-based backend.
